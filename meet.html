About Natalia

Science should feel exciting, real, and possible.

Hi! My name is Natalia, and I am the creator of Tiny Clues Science.

I am a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Illinois Chicago and I contribute to cryogenic research for the NASA-funded Terahertz Intensity Mapper mission.

The Terahertz Intensity Mapper is a telescope designed to be launched on a helium balloon about the size of a football stadium. My goal is to share and gain knowledge through every experiment, every project, and every mission.

Tiny Clues Science was created to help children experience the joy of discovery, curiosity, and hands-on science in a way that feels fun, safe, and unforgettable.
